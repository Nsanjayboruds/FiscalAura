# Sustainability and Documentation - TaxSathi

## 1. Sustainability Alignment (UN SDGs)

TaxSathi supports the following UN Sustainable Development Goals:

- **SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth**
  - Improves financial awareness and tax compliance for individuals.
  - Helps users optimize deductions and choose the better tax regime.

- **SDG 9: Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure**
  - Uses AI-assisted document analysis and tax strategy generation.
  - Demonstrates practical digital public-finance innovation.

- **SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities**
  - Makes tax guidance easier to understand for non-expert users.
  - Reduces access barriers to tax planning support.

- **SDG 16: Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions**
  - Encourages transparent and accurate tax filing behavior.
  - Supports stronger civic compliance through accessible tooling.
